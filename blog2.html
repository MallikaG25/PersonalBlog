Higher milk fat ice cream generally have a smoother texture because they contain a lower amount of water and, therefore, fewer ice crystals. Sugar or sugar substitutes are usually added to provide the sweet flavor that most people expect. No-sugar-added varieties of ice cream have become popular and rely on the addition of fruit and milk's natural sugars for their subtle sweetness.The variety of flavors and additives in ice cream has kept its popularity strong with consumers.
